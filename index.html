About Me

Born in Minsk, Belarus, and relocated to Minneapolis, I quickly embraced the vibrant mix of cultures and languages, setting the stage for a lifelong fascination with technology. From a young age, my curiosity had me dismantling toysâ€”not to play, but to uncover the secrets of their electronics. This early exploration ignited a deep-seated passion for I.T. and technological innovation.

During my middle school years, I channeled my interests into practical skills, mastering video editing while immersed in the world of gaming. This hobby not only provided endless entertainment but also became a bonding activity with my seven nephews, who share my enthusiasm for creative digital projects.

High school introduced new challenges and opportunities. To support my emerging interest in videography, I undertook various manual labor jobs, from landscaping to drywall. These roles taught me the value of hard work and perseverance, fueling my ambition and enabling me to invest in my first professional equipment.

My journey into professional videography began with sharing videos on YouTube and quickly expanded into automotive content creation for social media. What started as a hobby evolved into a career as I moved into freelance photography and real estate photography, and eventually became an FAA licensed and insured drone pilot.

Today, I continue to expand my expertise and explore new front-ends of technology. My latest ventures include delving into the world of coding for drone technology, aspiring to host mesmerizing drone light shows, and helping others establish their online presence through website creation. My journey is a testament to the power of passion melded with innovation at www.andreyzasulevich.com.

Whether you're a homeowner seeking an expert to showcase your property, a student curious about the intersections of technology and creativity, or a recruiter on the lookout for a dedicated lifelong learner with a diverse skill set, you'll find a committed partner in me.
